Setup Steps Windows Machine
  1. Download MT5 & Login
    • Download MT5 from the official website: metatrader5.com
    • Login with your broker’s User ID, Password, and Server (MetaTrader 5).
    • In MetaTrader, go to Tools → Options → Expert Advisors:
      • ✅ Allow Algorithmic Trading
      • ✅ Allow Web Request for listed URL → Add https://raw.githubusercontent.com
  2. Download the Algo Bot (dsq_v1)
    • Download the EA file: dsq_v1.ex5
    • Double‑click the downloaded file → it will be added to MetaTrader.
    • In the Navigator panel (right side), find dsq_v1 under Expert Advisors.
  3. Send your details for activation

    Submit your MetaTrader ID, Name, and Broker to get Licence.

    You can also message directly on the Telegram group (preferred).
dsq_V1 Bot Criteria & Risk – Read Carefully
If you have 10,000 INR (≈ 100 USD):
→ You need a Cent Account (not USD).
→ 100 USD in a cent account becomes 10,000 USC (the trading amount).
→ Minimum required: 100 USD deposit = 10,000 USC.

💰 Choose your account type

  • USD account: Needs at least 10,000 USD → not for small capital.
  • Cent account: Needs at least 100 USD → this is for 10,000 INR.
  • ⚠️ Always test on Demo first – same broker, same cent account type.
  • Run dsq_v1 only when market is sideways (flat, not trending one way).
  • 🆘 Emergency stop: In MetaTrader, press Ctrl+E once – the bot will stop immediately. Then manually close all existing trades.
  • ✅ Lower risk alternative: Try dsq_v2 or dsq_v3 – they include built‑in hedging and risk management.
